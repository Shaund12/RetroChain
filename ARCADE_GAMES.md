## ?? Scoring System

### Base Scoring
- Each game has its own scoring mechanics
- Scores are recorded on-chain
- High scores are permanently stored
- Leaderboards update in real-time

### Combo System
Combos multiply your score:
- 5 hits: 2x multiplier
- 10 hits: 3x multiplier
- 20 hits: 5x multiplier
- 50 hits: 10x multiplier
- 100 hits: 20x multiplier

### Token Conversion
- 1,000 points = Configurable RETRO tokens (default: 1 token)
- High score bonuses: 10-100 tokens
- Achievement rewards: 5-500 tokens
- Tournament prizes: 1,000-100,000 tokens

## ?? Tips for High Scores

### General Tips
1. **Learn the patterns** - Enemy patterns are consistent
2. **Use combos** - Chain kills for multipliers
3. **Save power-ups** - Use them strategically
4. **Practice** - Each continue improves your skill
5. **Watch the pros** - Study high score replays

### Genre-Specific Tips

**Shooters**
- Stay mobile
- Learn enemy spawn patterns
- Use the edges of the screen
- Don't waste power-ups on easy waves

**Platformers**
- Speed isn't everything
- Collect all coins
- Find secret areas
- Master the jump timing

**Puzzle**
- Think ahead
- Create chain reactions
- Don't rush
- Use power-ups before the board fills

**Fighting**
- Learn combos
- Block and counter
- Mix up your attacks
- Save special moves for clutch moments

**Racing**
- Learn the tracks
- Master drifting
- Use boost at optimal times
- Take the racing line

**Beat 'em Up**
- Crowd control is key
- Don't get surrounded
- Use your special attacks wisely
- Co-op makes it easier

**Maze**
- Learn ghost patterns
- Plan your route
- Save power pellets
- Clear corners carefully

**Pinball**
- Aim for multipliers
- Complete missions
- Keep the ball in play
- Learn the table layout
